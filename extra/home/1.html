This application is and will be used to certify student level data for State and Federal Reporting purposes. As new reports are added, they will appear in the dashboard below. To begin working on a particular collection, click on the name of that collection.

CHARTER SCHOOL REPORTING: All Charter School data has been converted to be reported through the charter school regardless of how the data is transmitted to the Wave. All charter schools will login via their "district" account to review, confirm, and certify the data. If you are a sponsoring school district, the charter school site will still appear in your list of school sites, but will not contain any data. Will have to "confirm" the empty site before you can certify your report.
