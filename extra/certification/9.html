Confirm/Release Sites - Site List

After verifying all of the information for a site is correct, confirm each site in your schools list. Once a site is marked "Confirmed", the data will no longer update. It will remain exactly as it was upon confirmation. If data corrections are necessary after a site was confirmed, the district superintendent will need to release the report. Releasing the report will update the data from what was in the Wave as of the last update time.
